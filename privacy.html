Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

Our Promise: Your privacy is our priority. We collect only what's necessary, encrypt everything, and never sell your data. Ever.

Introduction

Welcome to Remyndrs. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share information when you use our SMS-based reminder service. By using Remyndrs, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

What Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to provide our service:

Information You Provide

  • Phone Number: Required to send and receive SMS messages
  • Name and Email: Collected during onboarding for account identification and communication
  • ZIP Code: Used to determine your timezone for accurate reminder delivery
  • Message Content: Your reminder text, list items, and saved memories
  • Scheduled Times: When you want to receive reminders

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Interaction timestamps, message frequency, feature usage
  • Technical Data: Device type (inferred from carrier), message delivery status
  • Billing Information: Subscription tier, payment status (payment details handled by Stripe)

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for these purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Send reminders at the scheduled time in your timezone
  • Account Management: Manage your subscription, preferences, and account settings
  • Customer Support: Respond to your support requests and technical issues
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve natural language processing and features
  • Communication: Send service-related notifications (trial expiration, payment issues, etc.)

How We Protect Your Data

Encryption

  • In Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS encryption
  • At Rest: Sensitive personal information (names, emails) is encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption
  • Database Security: PostgreSQL database with encrypted connections and secure access controls

Access Controls

  • Limited employee access to user data on a need-to-know basis
  • Two-factor authentication for all administrative access
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

Data Retention

  • Active Reminders: Stored until delivered or deleted by you
  • Completed Reminders: Automatically deleted after 90 days
  • Memories & Lists: Retained until you delete them
  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active, deleted immediately when you text "DELETE ACCOUNT"

What We Don't Do

  • Never Sell Your Data: We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties
  • No Advertising: We don't serve ads or share data with advertising networks
  • No Tracking: We don't use cookies, pixels, or tracking scripts for advertising purposes
  • No Spam: We only send messages you explicitly request

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate Remyndrs. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data:

  • Twilio: SMS message delivery (covered by Twilio's Privacy Policy)
  • OpenAI: Natural language processing for understanding your messages (covered by OpenAI's Privacy Policy)
  • Stripe: Payment processing (covered by Stripe's Privacy Policy)
  • Render & Upstash: Infrastructure hosting and database services

Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

Access Your Data

  • Text "SHOW ALL" to see all your reminders
  • Text "SHOW LISTS" to view all your lists
  • Text "SHOW MEMORIES" to view saved memories
  • Text "STATUS" to see your account details

Delete Your Data

  • Delete individual reminders: Text "DELETE [reminder description]"
  • Delete lists: Text "DELETE [list name] LIST"
  • Delete all data: Text "DELETE ACCOUNT"

Export Your Data

  • Request a complete data export by emailing support@remyndrs.com
  • We'll provide your data in JSON format within 7 business days

Modify Your Preferences

  • Stop all messages: Text "STOP"
  • Manage your subscription: Text "ACCOUNT"

Children's Privacy

Remyndrs is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@remyndrs.com and we will delete it immediately.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you're a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of data collected, used, and shared
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of data sales (we don't sell data, so this doesn't apply)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

To exercise these rights, email support@remyndrs.com with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you're in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access: Obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restriction: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain data processing activities

To exercise these rights, email support@remyndrs.com with "GDPR Request" in the subject line.

Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will:

  • Notify you via SMS and email within 72 hours of discovery
  • Describe the nature of the breach and data affected
  • Explain steps we're taking to mitigate harm
  • Provide recommendations for protecting yourself

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes:

  • We'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • We'll notify you via SMS with a link to the new policy
  • Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance
