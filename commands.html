‚öôÔ∏è Account Commands

Important system commands

System Commands

  • HELP - Get help information, support contact details, and links to our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
  • STOP - Unsubscribe from receiving text messages from Remyndrs. This does not delete your information or your account. You can always text START to reactivate.
  • STATUS - View your account details, subscription tier, and usage stats.
  • UPGRADE or PREMIUM - Get a link to upgrade to Premium.
  • ACCOUNT - Get a link to manage your subscription and billing via the Stripe Customer Portal.
  • SUPPORT - Open a support ticket (Premium) or get support contact info.
  • FEEDBACK - Submit feedback or suggestions about Remyndrs.
  • BUG - Report a bug or issue with the service.
  • QUESTION - Ask a question about how to use Remyndrs.
  • SNOOZE - Snooze a reminder you just received (reply SNOOZE after getting a reminder).
  • SUMMARY ON / SUMMARY OFF - Enable or disable daily summary messages of your upcoming reminders.
  • DELETE ACCOUNT - Permanently delete all your data and cancel your subscription. Requires confirmation.
